Samaritan Medical Foundation is an organization dedicated to supporting the impovershed people of Nag Hammadi, Egypt, through medical care, resources, and spreading the Gospel.

Samaritan Medical Foundation is a compassionate organization committed to providing essential medical services and resources to the impoverished community of Nag Hammadi, Egypt. With a steadfast dedication to alleviating suffering and improving the quality of life, we offer vital healthcare assistance to those in need, regardless of their economic circumstances.


Our mission extends beyond physical well-being; we also strive to nurture spiritual growth and support individuals in their spiritual journey. Rooted in our Christian values, we believe in the transformative power of spreading the Gospel, offering hope, comfort, and guidance to those we serve.


Through collaborative efforts and partnerships, Samaritan Medical Foundation endeavors to create a healthier, more resilient community, where every individual has access to comprehensive healthcare and experiences the love and compassion of our organization. Together, we aim to make a meaningful difference in the lives of the underserved population of Nag Hammadi and foster a brighter, more hopeful future for all.
